Iced Pistachio Latte

Description

This iced pistachio latte, cool and refreshing, is made with only 3 ingredients.

The pistachio cream is sweet enough that no additional sweetener is needed.

Ingrediants

  • 1 tablespoon pistachio cream
  • 6 fluid ounces whole milk
  • ice, as needed
  • 2 shots espresso

Steps

  1. Swirl pistachio cream into a glass.
  2. Using a straw, push cream down and around.
  3. Add milk. Fill glass to within 1 inch of rim with ice. Pour in espresso.
